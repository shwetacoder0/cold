Frequently Asked Questions

How fast do you build MVPs?

Our standard MVP sprint is 2 weeks. For more complex projects, it might take 3-4 weeks. We prioritize speed without sacrificing quality.

What tech stack do you use?

We are technology-agnostic but typically use modern, scalable stacks like React/Next.js for frontend, Node.js/Python for backend, and deploy on AWS or Vercel.

Who's on the team?

Your project team will consist of a product manager, a UI/UX designer, and one or two senior developers, led by our founder.

How does onboarding work?

It starts with a discovery call, followed by a proposal. Once agreed, we'll set up a shared Slack channel and have a kickoff meeting to start the sprint.

What if I don't like the design?

We have a dedicated design sprint phase with multiple revision cycles to ensure you are 100% happy with the look and feel before we write a single line of code.
